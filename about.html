About PensaWay

PensaWay provides safe, reliable access to general aviation through aircraft rental, discovery flights, and introductory private pilot training. Our focus is on technical precision, operational safety, and supporting pilots at every stage of their journey.

Our Aircraft

We operate a Piper Cherokee PA‑28‑180, tail number N8440W. This aircraft is known for its stability, predictable handling, and suitability for both training and hour building.

Our Base – Roscoe Field (82J)

Located at 9750 Aileron Ave, Pensacola, FL, Roscoe Field (82J) offers convenient access to local and regional airspace, making it an ideal location for training flights and cross‑country operations.

Our Mission

To provide aspiring and licensed pilots with dependable aircraft access, structured learning opportunities, and a clear path toward achieving their aviation goals.
